# Proactive Intelligence

Koi doesn't wait for you to ask. It monitors your life in the background and reaches out when something needs your attention — through push notifications, SMS, or Telegram.

**And the longer you use it, the smarter it gets.**

Every conversation, every email, every notification you tap or ignore — Koi quietly learns from it all. After a week it knows your routine. After a month it knows your preferences. After six months it knows your life better than any app you've ever used.

---

## ☀️ Smart Weather Alerts

Koi checks the forecast twice a day and warns you **before** the weather changes — not after.

- "🌧️ Rain starting around 3 PM — grab an umbrella before you leave"
- "🥶 Temperature dropping 15° tonight, down to -2°C"
- Severe weather warnings (storms, high winds, extreme heat)
- **Silent when the weather is fine** — no unnecessary notifications

## 📅 Calendar Reminders

Koi scans your calendar every morning and creates smart reminders:

- **30 minutes before** each event
- **45 minutes before** if the event has a location (so you have travel time)
- **10 minutes before** if there are attendees (time to prepare)

## ✅ Task Nudges

Every morning, Koi checks your to-do list:

- Overdue tasks that need your attention today
- Tasks due today that you might forget
- Evening rollover — at 8 PM, suggests moving unfinished tasks to tomorrow

## 📧 Important Email Alerts

When a new email arrives, Koi reads it instantly and decides if you need to know:

- Verification codes and security alerts
- Payment failures and billing issues
- Time-sensitive actions and deadlines
- Personal urgent messages

**Learns your preferences:** Over time, Koi notices which email notifications you tap vs ignore, and automatically adjusts — silencing senders you don't care about and prioritizing the ones you always read.

## 📦 Package Tracking

When your package status changes, Koi notifies you immediately:

- "📦 Your Amazon package is out for delivery"
- "✅ Package delivered to your front door"
- Tracks across all major carriers (UPS, FedEx, USPS, DHL, and 3,000+ more)

## 💳 Subscription Renewal Alerts

Koi checks daily for subscriptions renewing in the next 3 days:

- "Netflix renews tomorrow — $15.99/month"
- "Your Adobe Creative Cloud annual plan renews in 3 days — $659.88"
- Automatically detected from your emails — no manual setup needed

## 🎂 Birthday & Important Date Reminders

Koi remembers the people in your life:

- **5 days before:** "Your mom's birthday is in 5 days — need gift ideas?"

## 🌙 Evening Summary

At the end of each day, Koi gives you a quick recap:

## 📋 Weekly Planning

Every Sunday evening, Koi helps you prepare for the week:

Koi learns your daily rhythm and adjusts itself:

- Discovers your peak morning and evening activity times

## 🧠 The Longer You Use It, The Smarter It Gets

Unlike ChatGPT — which forgets everything between conversations — Koi builds a permanent understanding of who you are, what you care about, and how you live. Here's how:

### Day 1 — It Reads Your Emails

The moment you connect Gmail or Outlook, Koi scans your inbox and learns:

- Your name, address, phone number, timezone
- Your job title and company
- Your family members and their birthdays
- Your airline loyalty programs (United MileagePlus, Delta SkyMiles...)
- Your car make and model
- Your active subscriptions (Netflix, Spotify, iCloud...)

> *You don't fill out a profile form. Koi figures it out from your emails — automatically.*

### Week 1 — It Remembers What You Tell It

Every time you mention a preference, Koi remembers it permanently:

- **"I prefer aisle seats"** → Next time you ask about flights, Koi recommends aisle seats and explains why
- **"Don't remind me about newsletters"** → Koi stops notifying you about newsletter emails
- **"My mom's name is Lisa"** → Koi remembers, and will use this context naturally in future conversations

These aren't just keywords in a database. Koi stores the *why* behind each fact and *when to apply it* — so it uses your preferences at the right moment, not randomly.

### Week 2 — It Learns Your Routine

Koi's Habit Discovery system analyzes your behavior patterns:

- **When you're most active** — If you always chat at 8am, morning notifications shift to 8am
- **What features you use most** — Heavy calendar user? Koi prioritizes calendar alerts
- **Your weekly rhythm** — Busy on Mondays, quiet on Fridays? Koi knows

> *Example: You've been checking your calendar every morning at 7:30am for two weeks. Koi automatically moves your morning briefing from 7:00 to 7:30 — right when you need it.*

### Month 1 — It Learns What Emails Matter to You

Koi watches which notifications you tap vs which ones you swipe away:

- **Ignored 3 times** → Koi silences that sender. No more interruptions.
- **Tapped 3 times** → Koi marks that sender as priority. Even if the email looks routine, you'll always be notified.

> *Example: You always tap emails from your boss but always ignore emails from HR newsletters. After a few weeks, Koi stops notifying you about HR newsletters — and makes sure your boss's emails always get through, even on weekends.*

### Month 3 — It Knows Your Life

By now, Koi has built a rich picture:

- Your travel preferences → "Book me a flight to NYC" → Koi searches United first (your loyalty airline), prefers aisle seats, and checks your calendar for conflicts
- Your family context → "What should I get my mom?" → Koi knows her birthday, your relationship, what you got her last year
- Your financial patterns → "How much did I spend this month?" → Koi knows your subscription costs, recent expenses, and spending trends
- Your daily rhythm → Morning briefings arrive exactly when you wake up, not at some generic 7am

### Month 6 — Your Personal AI, Truly Personal

At this point, Koi feels less like an app and more like a trusted assistant who genuinely knows you:

- Ask about flights → it already knows your airline, your seat preference, and checks your calendar
- Ask about gift ideas → it knows your mom's birthday, your relationship, your budget patterns
- Wake up in the morning → briefing arrives at exactly the right time, with exactly the info you need

**No other AI can do this** — because no other AI has spent six months learning who you are.

### How It Works (No Magic, Just Good Engineering)

Koi uses multiple memory layers that work together:

| Layer | What It Remembers | How Long |
|-------|-------------------|----------|
| **Conversation Memory** | What you're working on right now | Current session |
| **Long-Term Memory** | Facts and context from past conversations | Forever |
| **Structured Facts** | Explicit preferences with reasoning ("prefers aisle seats because...") | Forever |
| **Email Profile** | Identity, relationships, loyalty, vehicle info — extracted from your inbox | Forever, auto-updates |
| **Behavioral Patterns** | Your daily routine, feature usage, notification preferences | Refreshed weekly |
| **Notification Learning** | Which senders you care about vs ignore | Rolling 30 days |

When these layers conflict, structured facts always win — so if you explicitly say "I changed my mind, I prefer window seats now," Koi immediately overrides any old memories about aisle seats.
